Viking stew

Description

A true son of Odin can't finish a cold winter day without some hot, steaming stew to warm the belly.

Fortunately, stew requires only meat, vegetables, a hot fire and some patiance.

(Image and recipe credits to Pants Down, Aprons On).

Ingredients

  • One large yellow onion
  • Two large russet potates
  • One pound beef or venison
  • One mug of beer
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Drop everything in a big iron pot.
  2. Put the pot on the fire.
  3. Let it cook for a few hours, tasting every once in a while.
