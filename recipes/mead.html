Mead

Description

Mead is, quite literally, the drink of the gods. Made from pure honey, water, and yeast; fermented to perfection.

But great mead only comes to those who wait. A key principle in meadmaking is resting the mead after it is fully fermented. This resting period is crucial to reduce the amount of complex alcohols that are formed during the fermentation phase.

Ingredientes

  • Pure bee honey (preferably from a single origin)
  • Pristine water, fresh from the well
  • White wine yeast

Steps

  1. Mix honey with warm water in the desired proportion.
  2. After the mixture cools down, add yeast and mix well.
  3. Allow the mixture to ferment for two weeks (or until no yeast activity).
  4. Transfer to a new bottle and let it rest for two month, at least.
  5. Bottle the mead and allow it to rest in the bottle for two more months.
