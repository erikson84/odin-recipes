Dried fish

Description

You can't call yourself worth of Odin if you never ate dried fish. In the dry, cold northern climate, preserving fish without a fridge could be accomplished with ease.

Ingredients

  • Fresh white fish
  • Brining salt

Steps

  1. Scale and clean the fish.
  2. Important! When cleaning the fish, make sure to open the spine and clear the marrow.
  3. Apply a good coat of brining salt all over the fish.
  4. Hang the salted fish to dry in the shade for a few days.
